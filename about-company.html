About Us

We are Dream Big, a non-profit organization that aims to educate our commmunity about the college admissions process and ease the process for students across the nation.

Our Mission
Helping Students Dream Big

Our mission is to help high school students learn about the college admissions process early by providing them with a collaborative environment as well as a variety of interactive sessions that will allow them to easily gauge different aspects of high school and college.
