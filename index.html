Immerse yourself in a large collaborative environment.

Dream Big, a non-profit organization started small as in-person group of 20 students and parents in a sunroom with a primary mission to educate our community about the college admission process. Today, this has grown to a larger group with over 475 members and four high-school volunteers harnessing the power of social media. In this collaborative environment, parents and students can get their questions clarified by communicating with the engaging group members. Other free services we provide include panel discussions with high school and college students, a breakdown of the college admissions process, informational sessions with reputable college counselors, and valuable sessions from current & former college admissions officers.
